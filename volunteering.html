Heartfelt Service: My Commitment to Community

From Local Initiatives to Global Impact: My Journey of Service

Empowering Communities and Advocating Sustainability

As an active member of Societe Generale's CSR team, I have participated in various initiatives that not only enriched communities but also promoted environmental sustainability. From participating in the Akshayapatra initiative to serving nutritious meals to school children, to volunteering in lake cleaning drives, I have always strived to make a meaningful impact.

In my pursuit of a greener planet, I championed the 'Go Paperless' initiative, promoting digital alternatives to reduce paper consumption in the corporate environment. My commitment to environmental conservation further extended to the 'Plant Trees & Save Trees' campaign, where I actively contributed to afforestation efforts.

Beyond environmental causes, I have also been a vocal advocate for inclusivity and diversity. As a proud member of the pride community, I have participated in various Pride Marches, championing the rights and visibility of the LGBTQ+ community.

Through these volunteering experiences, I have not only contributed to societal welfare but also developed invaluable soft skills that complement my technical expertise in data science.

Championing Sustainability

I believe in leading by example when it comes to sustainability. As a speaker within SGGSC, I have advocated for reducing carbon footprints and adopting sustainable practices in corporate settings.

Inclusivity in Action

My involvement with the pride community has been a journey of empowerment and advocacy, fostering an inclusive environment for all.
